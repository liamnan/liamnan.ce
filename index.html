About Me

I'm a passionate engineer graduating with an M.S. in Aerospace Engineering in May 2026, with a strong foundation in spacecraft design, propulsion systems, compressible flow, and computer-aided design. I consider myself an expert problem solver who is uniquely creative and adaptable. I am constantly taking up new professional challenges and personal hobbies to keep my abilities fresh and my toolbox expanding. I am proficient in industry-standard software such as MATLAB, SolidWorks, ANSYS Fluent, Fusion, and Siemens NX. Throughout my academic and professional career, I have honed my skills in complex root-cause analysis, attention to detail, problem-solving, and data-driven decision-making. I am particularly interested in advancing space flight technologies, with a primary focus on exploration and interplanetary habitation. My dream is to eventually contribute to sending humans to live beyond Earth, and I want to work on the systems that bring them there. Beyond my technical expertise, I enjoy 3D design/printing, rug-making, woodworking, and rock climbing - hobbies that let me explore problem-solving in fun and hands-on ways. I am always looking for new ways to push my own boundaries, so that I can contribute to pushing the boundaries of aerospace technology and space exploration.
